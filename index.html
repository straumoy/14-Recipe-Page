Chocolate Chip Cookies

These homemade cookies are soft, chewy, and loaded with chocolate chips. Perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
