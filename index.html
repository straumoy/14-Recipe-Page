Chocolate Chip Cookies

These homemade cookies are soft, chewy, and loaded with chocolate chips. Perfect for any occasion!


Photo by Lisa from Pexels from Pexels.com

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F (175C)
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugars until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  4. In a seperate bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix well.
  6. Stirr in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
  9. Let cool before serving.
